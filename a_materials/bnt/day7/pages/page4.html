The <meta> tag is one of the most powerful and underappreciated tags in HTML. It does not display on the page, but browsers, search engines, social media, and devices use it heavily to interpret and optimize your site.

1. <meta charset="UTF-8">

Purpose: Defines the character encoding. UTF-8 supports almost every character from all human languages, including Tamil, emojis, symbols, etc.

7. <meta http-equiv="refresh">

Purpose: Auto-refresh or redirect a page after a time interval.

8. <meta http-equiv="X-UA-Compatible">

Purpose: Forces Internet Explorer to use the latest rendering engine. 

<meta http-equiv="X-UA-Compatible" content="IE=edge">

9. Social Media: Open Graph Tags

Purpose: Enhances how your page looks when shared on Facebook, LinkedIn, WhatsApp.

11. <meta name="theme-color">

Purpose: Sets browser color bar in mobile devices.
